Mr. Anil Kumar Gupta

MD, IPRCL

Mr. Anil Kumar Gupta

Ideas Forum

Mr. Anil Kumar Gupta has passion for innovation, habbit of taking initiatives and working mostly in Startup Organizations & Units since 1998 beginning with Delhi Metro Project. Belong to IRSE 1987 batch, having 31 year experience in diverse fields in open line, construction and workshop on Indian Railways, Delhi Metro Project, IRCON RITES, RLDA and NTPC and at present building IPRCL. Since completing mid career PGDPPM in 2006 I have worked mostly with PPP projects beginning as Director PPP at the Ministry of Railways in year 2006. I have experience of 5 year at Delhi Metro Project in Phase-I, and 6 year commercial development of land at RLDA. Have extensive Board level experience since 2015 having been part time directors at seven Joint Venture/subsidiary Companies of IRCON dealing with PPP projects including IRSDC including creating 4, IRSDC, MCRL, JCRL and BRPL of them. Extensive experience in designing, planning, procurement and management in EPC/PMC /PPP/Leasing contracts and consultancies. He was associated in development of model RFQ/RFP for PPP projects and model PMC document/guidelines for railways. He have worked with international experts, consultants and contractors. He take pride and keen interest in sharing my knowledge and experience as Visiting Lecturer on PPP, Project Management and Contract Management to IRICEN Pune, NIFM Fridabad, IRTM Lucknow, MDI Gurgaon. I am fond of writing down my experiences and so far have authored/co-authored 13 technical/management papers including 'PPP in Railways-A New Approach' published in IIMB Management Review in March 2008 and 'Project Management Control System of Infrastructure SPVs:DMRC- A Case Study' published in Book Public Private Partnerships, by Routledge Taylor & Francis Group in 2010.

Building India Inc. is a platform that brings together the country's greatest minds in business, economics, and innovation to discuss issues that touch the lives of all Indians. The Business Summit is known for raising topics of contemporary Indian relevance, discussing possible solutions to problems voiced by practitioners, and deliberating best practices to translate India's experiences to propel remarkable growth.
